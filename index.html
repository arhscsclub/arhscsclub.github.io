About ARHS CS

Computer Science Club at Auburn Riverside High School meets in room 804 every Wednesday morning at 7:30 AM. Our advisor is computer science teacher, math teacher, and soccer coach Mrs. Robyn Saarenas.

Feel free to come join us! Free donuts are provided and the only thing you need to bring is a curiosity to learn.
